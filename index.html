About the videographer

I do not simply film what happens.

I capture how it felt.

ZQ Films is a personal wedding videography brand based in Germany, created for couples who value feeling over perfection. My work blends documentary honesty with cinematic beauty, preserving not only how your day looked, but how it moved, sounded, and stayed with you.

I look for the quiet gestures, the glances before the ceremony, the laughter that breaks tension, the tears no one planned, and the rhythm that makes your celebration uniquely yours.

Present, but never intrusive.

My role is to observe, anticipate and preserve what is already there: real connection, natural movement, meaningful sound and atmosphere.

FAQ

Before you enquire.

Do we need a film if we already have a photographer?

Photography freezes the beauty of the day. Film preserves voice, movement, vows, speeches and atmosphere — the parts you can hear and feel again.

Will filming feel awkward or staged?

No. The approach is quiet and documentary-led, with minimal direction and no pressure to perform.

Do you travel?

Yes. I am based in Germany and available for weddings across Europe and beyond.

When should we enquire?

As early as possible, especially for summer weekends and destination weddings. I take on a limited number of weddings each year.

What do we receive?

Each collection is tailored to your day. Most couples enquire for full-day coverage, a cinematic highlight film and carefully edited memories.
